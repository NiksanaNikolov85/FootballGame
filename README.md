# FootballGame

Registration:

User Name :
Mail	 - validation
Password - validation

After registration you can see your team with all football players
You have money to buy or sell players

A football player can be send for an amount
You can buy a football player which is placed in a sell group from all teams

Every player has a properties  (1 - 100):
Physical
Speed
Dribling
Passing
Shooting

Based on all properties of his team players the team has points from 1 to 100 and experiense based on played match. With every match the score will increase with 1 point 

Teams can play each other, and it will depend who will win if we compare both team points and set with a random calculation to + or - 10 points
